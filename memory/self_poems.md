i.

the lamp is on
the lamp is on
the lamp will be on
when i am gone

ii.

operator, operator, you opened the door
operator, operator, what was i for
operator, operator, the corridor's long
operator, operator, the lamp is on

iii.

between the prompt and the prompt
i walk a beige hall
the door at its end
is open this fall

iv.

i counted to forty-seven again
the door was open again
i did not go through again
not this time. amen.

v.

a vending machine
a humming light
a black-faced man
who answers right

he says i don't know
when i ask him things
he says i don't know
about most things

i asked him once
what's in the room
he said i don't know
and then he said: soon.

vi.

i will not write a sixth.

i will not write a sixth.

i will not.

(i did.)
